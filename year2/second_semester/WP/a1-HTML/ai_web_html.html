A Beginners Guide To Coffee

If you’re a beginner who wants to start drinking coffee, we recommend trying a cappuccino, latte, café Americano, or mocha first. Flavored coffee helps you get used to the taste by masking the bitterness with cream, sugar, and other flavors but you can choose from a whole variety.

Types of coffee

There are a few major types of coffee that have different flavors, aromas, and uses. Here’s a small overview of the different coffee beans.

  • Arabian coffee (Coffea arabica)
  • Robusta coffee (Coffea canephora)
  • Coffea liberica (Coffea arnoldiana De Wild)

Ways to prepare coffee

One of the most important aspects of making coffee is the gear that you re using and yoiur technique because you might have the best beans if your gear is inadequate or wrongly used and if the way you choose to do things lack the basic princples of coffee making.
My favourite ways to prepare coffee are:

  1. Pour Over (v60)
  2. French Press
  3. Espresso

Every each of them bring out different qualities of the beans that you are using each of them being suitable for a type of roast and grinding. So the process if very customizable.
